## Components of Graph

- **Vertices:** Vertices are the fundamental units of the graph. Sometimes, vertices are also known as vertex or nodes. Every node/vertex can be labeled or unlabelled.
- **Edges:** Edges are drawn or used to connect two nodes of the graph. It can be ordered pair of nodes in a directed graph. Edges can connect any two nodes in any possible way. There are no rules. Sometimes, edges are also known as arcs. Every edge can be labeled/unlabelled.
- **Path**: Edges between two Nodes.
- **Adjacent Nodes**: Two nodes are called Adjacent if they are connected through an edge.
- **Directed-Graph**: A directed graph is one where the edges can be traversed in specific direction only.
- **Weight**: Weight is one where the edges have given a value.
- **Length**: Number of edges connected to that node.

## Representation of Graph

- **Adjacency matrix**: Graph in 2-D Array.
- **Adjacency lists**: Graph in Linked lists.

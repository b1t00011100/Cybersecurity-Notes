## Equations

- Relative Frequency = f/N (f-Frequency of that Class, N- Total Frequency)
- % Relative Frequency = f/N*100 (f-Frequency of that Class, N- Total Frequency)
- Density Frequency = f/h (f-frequency of that class , h - class width)

# Chapter 3 : Developing Positive Attitude

- **What is attitude ? Explain with example**

→ Attitude is a mental state involving beliefs, feelings an a tendency to act in a certain way towards people, situations or things

It includes :

- How we think
- How we feel
- How we react

Example : Viewing failure as a learning opportunity shows a positive attitude.

- **What are the characteristics of attitude ?**

→ 

1. Everyone holds attitudes regardless of age, gender or background
2. Attitudes influence decisions and behavior
3. They can be positive , negative or neutral
4. Attitudes are learned and can be learned 
5. They are shaped by experience, environment and thinking
6. Though internal, attitudes become visible through actions

- **Differentiate between attitude and behaviour**

→

| **Attitude** | **Behaviour** |
| --- | --- |
| Internal Mindset | External action |
| What you think/ feel | What you do |
| Based on belief and emotions | Shaped by socail norms / situations |
| Hidden from others | Observable by others |
| Can exist without being shown | Always visible |

- **What are the factors responsible for formation of attitude ?**

→

1. **Family -** Early values and beliefs are formed at home
2. **Peer Group -** Friends influence ideas and opinions
3. **Media -** TV, social media and films shape thinking
4. **Social Factors -** Religion, culture, locality impact attitudes
5. **Direct Instructions -** Knowledge from teachers, elders or leaders
6. **Personal Experience -** Positive/negative events leave emotional marks
7. **Prejudice -** Pre formed opinions (Positive or negative)
8. **Economic Conditions -** Financial status affects thinking

- **Can attitudes be changed ? How ?**

→ Yes, attitudes can be changed over time due to :

- New experiences
- Social Interactions
- Exposure to new information
- Aware and reflection
    
    A person can adopt a positive attitude by learning, observing, and practicing better thinking patterns.
    
- **What is the impact of a positive attitude?**

→

- Boosts self-confidence and motivation
- Improves relationships and teamwork
- Helps overcome challenges calmly
- Promotes mental and physical health
- Enhances productivity and job satisfaction
    
    A positive attitude often leads to a more successful and happy life.
    

- **Give examples of positive and negative attitudes.**

→ 

**— Positive Attitudes:**

- Optimism
- Gratitude
- Acceptance
- Willingness to learn

**— Negative Attitudes:**

- Complaining
- Blaming others
- Refusing change
- Always expecting failure

# Chapter 1 : Soft Skills

- **What are soft skills ?**

→ Soft skills are interpersonal qualities and emotional intelligence traits like communcation, teamwork, leadership and adoptability. They complement hard (technical) skills and are crucial for workplace success.

- **Why are soft skills important for career success ?**

→ Soft skills help in effective communication, teamwork, leadership and adaptability. Employers value them highly as they influence work relationships and performance, making them essential to secure and retain jobs.

- **Differentiate between soft skills and hard skills ?**

→ Hard skills - Techincal measurable (e.g programming, cerificates )

    Soft skills - Interpersonal emotional ( e.g communication , leadership )

- **How can one sell soft skills in interviews?**

→ By selecting relevant soft skills, giving real-life examples to support them, and explaining how          these skills benefit the employer. Confidence, storytelling, and relevance to the job are key.

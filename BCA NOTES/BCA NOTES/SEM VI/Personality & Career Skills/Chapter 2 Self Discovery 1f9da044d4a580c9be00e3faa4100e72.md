# Chapter 2 : Self Discovery

- **What is self-discovery ?**

→ Self discovery is the process of understanding one’s own personality, strengths, weaknesses, emotions and values. It helps in making better life decisions and achieving personal growth.

- **Explain the importance of knowing yourself ?**

→ Knowing yourself leads to better decision making, self control, awareness of strengths/ weakness and helps in setting realistic goals. It boosts self confidence and personal growth.

- **What is SWOT analysis in self discovery ?**

→ SWOT stands for strengths, weaknesses, Opportunities and threats. It is a method used to analyze personal capabilities and challenges to improve self-understanding and career planning.

- **How can one improve self-awareness ?**

→ By introspection, receiving feedback, using tools like Johari Window or personality tests, and reflecting on one’s thoughts and behaviors regularly.

- **Describe the process of knowing oneself ?**

→ 

- Understand your personality
- Identify core values
- Know your body and limitations
- Discover your dreams
- Reflect on your likes, dislikes, and goals

- **How do you create a SWOT analysis grid ?**

→ Divide a table into 4 parts :

- **Strenghts :** your abilities an advantages
- **Weakness :** your flaws or areas to improve
- **Opportunities :** external chances to grow
- **Threats :** external challenges or risks

- **What are the three main body types ?**

→ 

1. **Ectomorph -** Thin, hard to gain weight
2. **Mesomorph -** Muscular, athletic
3. **Endomorph -** Higher fat, gains weight easily

- **What role do dreams play in self-discovery ?**

→ Dreams reflect inner desires and long-term goals. Following your dreams helps set personal motivation and leas to meaningful growth. They are key motivators.
